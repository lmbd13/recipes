
    SIMPLE FRIED RICE

• 2 cups cooked rice (cold/day-old)
• 2 eggs
• 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
• 2 tbsp soy sauce
• 2 tbsp oil
• 2 spring onions, chopped

1. Heat 1 tbsp oil, scramble eggs, set aside
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil (high heat), add vegetables
3. Stir-fry vegetables (2-3 min)
4. Add rice, break up clumps, stir-fry (3 min)
5. Add soy sauce, eggs, spring onions
6. Toss everything (1 min)

⏱ Time: 10 minutes
